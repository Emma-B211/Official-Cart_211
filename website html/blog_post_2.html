About My First Project


This project was created for my final semester course that would determine if I passed the program.


Before the pandemic and lockdown, my family and I went to see a light show that occurs in the notre dame basilica called the Aura Experience. I had such an amazing time and experience of seeing a light show along side music and I grew inspired to recreate an art installation that would emulate the same experience that I went through.

I used wood planks and cylinder rods for the main frame of the tent, dark fabric that would provide cover, plastic holders for the light strips.
light strips bought from amazon, screws, and white mesh covering provided from the class material to diffuse the light which would be controlled through the code on a microchip.
